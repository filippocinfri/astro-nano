_"Shh. Don't breathe too hard and don't speak; you are exhaling humid air. You'll put out the candle."_

"What? You just threw a bucket of water toward the candle."

_"The flame is so little now, you have to stop speaking, or you'll put it out."_

"What? You are holding the still-dripping bucket. Why did you throw a full bucket at the bright candle?"

_"I told you to stop. Now there is no flame anymore. I tried to tell you to stop producing moist air. I was trying so hard, I told you twice, and you didn't listen to me. I was trying to help. You didn't listen to what I told you. What were you doing while I was trying to help? You put it out."_

The candle was bright, and it was really nice to see. It looked like the flame was dancing hypnotically. I enjoyed this show.

Now the stage, the floor, is wet. And the flame, the main show, is off.

Will it ever be on again? Anyone in the room could turn it on again.

"Hey, someone in this room should turn the candle on again."

_"It's unhealthy keeping you around. You weren't even sorry. You should leave the room. You view my actions poorly, but I know the intentions behind my actions. I know myself. My friends told me I am a good person."_
